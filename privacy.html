PrivacyPolicy

Last updated: October 2023

01. Introduction

Beijing Zhishenglongcheng Information Technology Co., Ltd. ("ZHISHENG") is a global leader in mobile applications and game services. We are dedicated to protecting the privacy of our users. This policy outlines our rigorous data practices across all mobile applications and digital services.

02. Global Age Management & Protection

ZHISHENG adheres strictly to global age-appropriateness standards. Our policies are specifically designed to comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), GDPR-K (General Data Protection Regulation for Kids), and local rating systems (ESRB, PEGI, CERO).

We implement a comprehensive global age management framework:

  • Age Verification: Strict age-gating mechanisms are integrated into all self-developed products to ensure content is appropriate for the target audience.
  • Child Protection: We do not knowingly collect personal information from users under the age of 13 (or the local age of consent) without verifiable parental consent. If such data is detected, it is immediately purged from our servers.
  • Default Privacy: For minor users, data collection is disabled by default, and personalized advertising is strictly prohibited across all our monetization frameworks.

03. Privacy Data Management & Compliance

We take a "Privacy by Design" approach to all data handling. Our systems are adapted for the following global standards:

  • GDPR (EU): We provide full transparency regarding data controllers, right to access, right to rectification, and the "Right to be Forgotten."
  • CCPA (California): We respect the rights of California residents to opt-out of the sale of personal information and provide comprehensive "Do Not Sell" mechanisms.
  • Store Policy Adaptation: Our data practices are audit-ready and fully aligned with the Google Play Data Safety Section and Apple App Store Privacy Nutrition Labels.

04. Third-Party Monetization & Data Processing

When integrating with global monetization platforms (AdMob, Unity Ads, AppLovin, etc.), we ensure that all data transmissions are encrypted (TLS/SSL) and compliant with the SDK provider's privacy protocols. We only share anonymized device identifiers (IDFA/GAID) as permitted by user consent frameworks.

05. Data Subject Requests

For any inquiries regarding your data, or to exercise your privacy rights, please contact our Data Protection Office at support@zhishenglc.com.
